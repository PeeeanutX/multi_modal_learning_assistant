AI-based business information systems have an increasing 
capacity to act on their own, without human intervention (and 
sometimes even without human knowledge!)
•
Examples:
–
AI-driven credit underwriting software that automatically makes 
credit and loan decisions
–
Robotic process automation (RPA) bots that log into ERP 
systems and automatically execute business processes
–
Robo-advisor platforms that automatically rebalance investments
–
…
