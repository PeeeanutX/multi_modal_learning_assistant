•
AI-based business information systems and their outputs often are 
intelligible only to a select audience (e.g., developers, data 
scientists)
•
For others, such as managers or business users, they can be a 
“black box”
•
Examples:
–
AI-based credit and loan decisions that are difficult to understand for 
financial advisors in a bank
–
AI-based sales forecasts that are difficult to understand for sales 
managers 
–
AI-based therapy recommendations that are difficult to understand 
for medical professionals
–
…
