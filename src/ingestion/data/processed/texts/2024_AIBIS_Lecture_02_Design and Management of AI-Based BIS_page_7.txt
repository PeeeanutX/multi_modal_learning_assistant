There are many process models or frameworks that offer step-by-step guidance on how to 
design a (business) information system
•
These models typically involve a set of activities:
–
Analyzing the status quo and collecting requirements
–
Evaluating (design) alternatives
–
Developing the system (or procuring it) 
–
Implementation, operation, and maintenance
–
…
•
The design process is not linear but iterative!
Prof. Dr. Ulrich Gnewuch
Chair of Explainable AI-based Business Information Systems
