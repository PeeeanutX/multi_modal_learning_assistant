Key Takeaways From This Lecture
•
Conversational AI is a major application area of AI-enabled 
engagement 
•
The first chatbot (Eliza) was already developed in 1966
•
Since then, the technology has evolved significantly: from 
rule-based to NLP-based to LLM-based conversational AI
•
Many different types of conversational (AI) agents exist
•
They can be classified based on the mode of 
communication (text vs. voice) and the context (open-
domain vs. task-oriented)
•
Conversational agents can be equipped with virtual 
embodiments (avatars) or physical embodiments (robots)
