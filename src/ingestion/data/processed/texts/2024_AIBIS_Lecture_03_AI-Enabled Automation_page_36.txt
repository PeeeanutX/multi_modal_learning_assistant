Skill erosion refers to the process of losing 
skills and knowledge needed to perform a 
job/task when automation takes over
•
Examples:
–
Financial advisors are no longer able to analyze 
loan applications and make loan decisions 
independently
–
AI writing assistance leads to a decline in writing 
skills
–
Generative AI leads to an erosion of human 
creativity (e.g., ChatGPT writes poems or essays)
–
…
