Standards are usually set by national or 
international bodies to ensure compliance with 
a set of design rules by a large community
•
Various standards exist for hardware and 
software (see ISO 13.180 Ergonomics 
https://www.iso.org/ics/13.180/x/)
•
Examples:
– ISO 9241: Ergonomics of human-system 
interaction
– ISO 6385:2016: Ergonomics principles in the 
design of work systems
