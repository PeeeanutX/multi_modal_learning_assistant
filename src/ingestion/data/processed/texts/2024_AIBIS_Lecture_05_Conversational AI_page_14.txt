Main components:
–
Speech recognition: converts the speech 
input into a string of words 
–
Natural language understanding: interpret 
the user’s input and to extract a representation 
of its meaning (→“intent”)
–
Dialog management: tracks the state and flow 
of the conversation and controls how the 
system responds to the user’s input
–
Natural language generation: Formulate a 
response in natural language
–
Text-to-speech synthesis: converts the 
response string into speech output
