Automation vs. Augmentation
Raisch & Krakowski 2021; Rai et al. 2019
Automation: Humans hand over a task 
to AI with little or no further involvement.
Augmentation: Humans collaborate 
closely with AI to perform a task.
Reduce costs and free up staff 
for more value-added work
Leverage complementary strengths 
and enable mutual learning
